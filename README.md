 # EleGuard 
 
**Smart Elephant Intrusion Alert & Prediction System**
 
*Protecting Sri Lankan farmers — one alert at a time*

</div>

 
## 📖 Overview
 
**EleGuard** is a mobile application that warns farmers when wild elephants are approaching their land — *before it's too late*.
 
Using signals from ground sensors placed around the farm boundary, EleGuard detects elephant movement in real time and instantly alerts the farmer on their phone — providing a smart, affordable first line of defence.
 
---
 
## 😟 The Problem
 
In rural Sri Lanka, elephant intrusions into farmland are a **serious and ongoing crisis**:
 
- 🌾 Crops are destroyed overnight
- 🏠 Property is damaged beyond repair
- 🧑‍🌾 Farmer lives are put at risk
- 🐘 Elephants are harmed in retaliatory conflicts
Existing solutions like electric fences are **expensive and often unreliable**, leaving farmers with no early warning when an elephant approaches. EleGuard was built to change that.
 
---
 
## 🔬 How It Works
 
When an elephant walks, it creates **seismic vibrations** in the ground. Geophone sensors buried along the farm boundary detect these vibrations and transmit the data to EleGuard, which classifies the threat level in real time.
 
```
🐘 Elephant walks → Ground vibrations → Geophone sensors → EleGuard app → Farmer alert
```
 
### Threat Level Classification
 
| Level | Indicator | Meaning |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| **LOW** | 🟡 Yellow | Elephant detected — far from boundary |
| **MEDIUM** | 🟠 Orange | Elephant approaching — stay alert |
| **HIGH** | 🔴 Red | Elephant at the boundary — **act now!** |
 
> 📄 **Research basis:** *Wijayaraja et al., IEEE Access, vol. 13, 2025*
 
---
 
## ✨ Key Features
 
- 📡 **Real-time detection** via geophone seismic sensors
- 🔔 **Instant push notifications** to the farmer's phone
- 📊 **3-level threat classification** (Low / Medium / High)
- 🗺️ **Boundary mapping** to pinpoint intrusion location
- 📈 **Movement prediction** to anticipate elephant paths
- 🔋 **Low-power sensor design** for rural deployment

> *To give every Sri Lankan farmer a smart, affordable, and reliable early warning system that protects their crops, their family, and the elephants — right from their phone.*
 
EleGuard is built with one clear mission: **zero preventable human-elephant conflicts**.
